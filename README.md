

HealthPilot AI is a personal health assistant web application designed to empower individuals to take proactive control of their health by predicting diabetes risk, providing early warnings, and suggesting personalized diet plans based on key health metrics.

The application integrates a **Random Forest Classifier** trained on health indicators such as glucose levels, BMI, and blood pressure to assess an individual’s risk of developing diabetes. By inputting their recent health data, users receive immediate feedback, including:

- 🚨 **Risk Assessment Alerts**: Clear, intuitive alerts indicating whether the user is at low, moderate, or high risk of diabetes, enabling timely intervention.
  
- 🍽️ **Personalized Diet Suggestions**: Tailored recommendations to help manage or reduce risk, supporting healthier lifestyle choices.

- 📊 **Interactive Health Dashboard**: Built using Plotly, Matplotlib, and Seaborn, the dashboard visualizes health trends over time with histograms, scatter plots, and heatmaps, making complex data easy to understand.

- 🗂️ **CSV-Backed Health History**: Tracks the last 10 user entries, preserving health data over time for comparison and progress tracking.

HealthPilot AI’s easy-to-use interface is built with **Streamlit**, allowing seamless interaction without any technical knowledge required. The application promotes **preventive care**, helping users detect health issues early, understand their health status, and make informed decisions.

**Key objectives of HealthPilot AI:**
- 📅 Encourage regular health monitoring.
- ⏱️ Provide quick, actionable insights into diabetes risk.
- 📉 Help reduce the prevalence of diabetes through early detection and education.
- 🔎 Offer an engaging, interactive experience to keep users motivated in their health journey.

Whether for personal use or as a prototype for health tech startups, HealthPilot AI demonstrates how machine learning and data visualization can come together to make preventive healthcare accessible and user-friendly.
